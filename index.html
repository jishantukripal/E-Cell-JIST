About Us

E-Cell JIST is the Entrepreneurship Cell of Jorhat Institute of Science and Technology.

E-Cell Tagline: AWARE | EXECUTE | IMPACT

Its vision is to develop, uplift, and enhance the inner entrepreneurs of the students of the Jorhat Institute of Science and Technology. It will provide the opportunity to present their ideas, networking, industry, and boost entrepreneurial thinking , which will lead to the creation of successful campus startups, creating entrepreneurs.
